# Cold Email — Tech Press Pitch

**Posting notes:** Send T+72h, after HN + Reddit traction is visible. Reporters want narratives backed by numbers — having "1,200 GitHub stars in 72 hours" or "top of HN for 4 hours" in the email dramatically raises reply rate. Send one email per outlet, never a BCC blast. Target reporters who have written about local LLMs, indie dev tools, or the changing job market in the last 60 days — read their recent bylines first.

**Suggested targets (by beat, not by name — research current bylines before sending):**
- TechCrunch: AI / developer tools reporter
- The Information: enterprise AI or future-of-work beat
- The Verge: AI tools or labor reporter
- Ars Technica: open-source / developer tools reporter
- Pragmatic Engineer (Gergely Orosz) newsletter — different format but high-leverage if it lands

---

## Subject line options (A/B in your head, pick one)

1. `New job category emerging: AI-native operator. Built an OSS tool for them.`
2. `Open-source tool: local-LLM job scorer (1.2k GH stars in 72 hours)`
3. `The job-search funnel is breaking — here's the open-source alternative that hit HN front page this week`

Recommendation: option 2 if traction numbers are real by send time; option 1 if not.
